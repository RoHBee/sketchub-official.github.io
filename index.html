About Sketchub

Sketchub is the first unofficial app created for sharing sketchware projects and apps to the world. By uploading your projects and apps on Sketchub you can get feedbacks from the users about how's your app or project is or how can you improve it. You can get a good user base in your app and there are many more advantages as Sketchub has 50-100 daily active users and an user base of 1k+ users.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get the Verified user badge?

For becoming a Verified user you should get nearly 100 total likes on profile and your all projects should follow our T&C after you'll complete this Target we'll review your profile and projects and if we find everything okay then we'll give you a Verified badge. Otherwise you can get verified badge manually too.

Why ads? How can I remove ads?

Who loves ads? Nobody, not even us. But servers are exhausting day by day, and financial support is necessary to run Sketchub smoothly. We have a Donation Button, if sufficient amount will be received, ads will be removed for all users.

What other benefits does a VIP user has?

1) You'll not face ads 2) A VIP badge will show in your profile. 3) A verified tick will show on your all projects. 4) Unlocked paid features which are not available yet.

Why my project is deleted?

Your project can be deleted by us if we found it against T&C, if you think it was a mistake you can contact us for avoiding further problems.

Am I allowed to upload apps made which are not through Sketchware?

No, Apps which are made only through sketchware are allowed, exceptionally you can upload apps which helps in Sketchware users in making apps.

Is Sketchub made through Sketchware?

Yes

Why Sketchub is not supported to Kitkat devices?

Kitkat Version is limited, most of java codes are not supported to it, especially Encryption and decryption codes, which are most important for security and we can't remove it.
