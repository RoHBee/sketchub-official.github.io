Sketchub servers are closed again.

  • Home
  • Blog
  • Notification
  • 12.02.2020

Hello Everyone, Sketchub servers are closed again but earlier.

Sketchub is being used by 100 active users at very high demand.

Since sketchub is free, no income is earned by us, even through ads, the income is almost zero. Some guys are overusing sketchub enough that other users got unable to use it. So this time we are going to be very strict with data usage so that everybody will have access to projects at moderate level.

In upcoming updates, we may apply video-ad for downloading projects, previously we closed video-ads because we were getting complaints that users have to watch ad again and again, but we are really sorry that we are going to add video-ads because some guys won't stop overusing sketchub if wouldn't take any steps. We may also restrict the uploading of projects, i.e. 10 projects/apps per user. Not only that, vip users, who are interested in donation, will get unlimited slots for uploading projects.

Hope that you guys will understand our problem, because we can't handle such demand without income for the servers.
